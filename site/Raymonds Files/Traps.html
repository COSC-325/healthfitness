Trap Workouts



Barbell Shrug

Grab a barbell with your hands just beyond shoulder length apart. Let the bar hang infornt of your waist then shrug your shoulders has high as you can go and really squeeze at the top. Then slowly bring the bar back down to the starting position and repeat.

Dumbbell Shrug

Grap two dumbbells ad let them hang along your side. Then shrug your shoulders as high as you can. Once you reach the top really squeeze your traps then slowly bring it back down to the starting position. Then repeat.

Barbell Behind-the-Back Shrug

This is the same exercise as the barbell shurg but you are just moving the bar to the back of you.

Dumbbell Overhead Carry

Grab a dumbbells in each of your hands. Then press them over your head and walk forward. Do this until your traps are getting worn out.

Dumbbell Lateral Raise

Grap a pair of dumbells and let your hands hang to the sides of your body. Then slowly bring thr weights up to your shoulders. You should form a T-shape. Hold there for a second or two then slowly bring them back to the starting position. Then repeat.
