Hamstring Workouts



Deadlift

Grap a bar and set it down infront of your feet. Have your legs shoulder width apart and grap the bar. Pull the bar up but make sure your keep your back totally straight to prevent any back injury. Once you stand up slowly bring the bar back down to the ground and repeat.

Machine Leg Curl

Lay down at the machine and adjust the pad so it is laying on your achilles. Lift the bar up so you make a 90 degree angle with your legs. Then slowly bring the bar back down to the starting position and repeat.

Glute Ham Raise

Adjust the foot plate back so the when you lie on it the pad will be right at your hips. Slowly lean forward until the pad reaches your hips then slowly bring your body back to the starting position. On the way up, make sure your really squeeze your hamstrings.

Walking Lunges

Grap two dumbbells and hold one in each of your hands. Take one step and lower down into a lunge with your knee almost hitting the ground. Stand back up and then do it with your other leg.
